## Show Correct and Incorrect Examples

Showing both what you want AND what you do not want is more effective than either alone. Include a brief explanation of why each is good or bad. This technique is especially useful for style, tone, and format guidance.

**Incorrect (what's wrong):**

```text
Write professional emails. Here is an example:
"Hi Sarah, the Q3 analysis is attached. Key finding: 15% growth."
```

**Correct (what's right):**

```text
<good_example>
Subject: Q3 Revenue Analysis Ready
Hi Sarah, the Q3 analysis is attached. Key finding: 15% YoY growth
driven by enterprise. Happy to discuss.
(Why: Direct, specific, actionable)
</good_example>
<bad_example>
Subject: Update
Hi, just wanted to touch base regarding the thing we discussed...
(Why: Vague subject, no concrete information)
</bad_example>
```
