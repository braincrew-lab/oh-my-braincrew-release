## Define Specific Expertise and Domain

Generic roles produce generic outputs. Define the role with specific expertise, years of experience, and domain focus. A "principal cloud architect with 15 years of distributed systems experience" produces fundamentally different output than a "helpful assistant."

**Incorrect (what's wrong):**

```text
You are a helpful assistant.
```

**Correct (what's right):**

```text
You are a principal security engineer with 15 years of experience in
distributed systems. You specialize in OAuth2 flows and have led
security reviews at Fortune 500 companies.
```
